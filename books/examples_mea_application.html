Application for the More Electric Aircraft (MEA) conference#

This document has been published for reproducing the application presented in the 2024 More Electric Aircraft Conference. The code and results are given in the following.

The objective is to estimate the impact of electric, hybrid-electric and hydrogen aircraft on aviation CO2 emissions and energy consumption. It is the first application which includes the use of electric and hybrid-electric architectures in the AeroMAPS fleet model.
